Chana Masala (Chickpea Curry)

Info

Note - You should have all of the ingredients measured and ready before you begin cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Onion, chopped small
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Chilli Pepper (Serano or whatever is available), chopped small
  • 1.5~2 inches of Ginger, peeled and minced
  • 2 cans Chickpeas, drained
  • 1 small can Tomato Puree or Paste
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Coconut Flour (optional)
Spices:
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon Mustard Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Coriander
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Cumin
  • 4 Curry Leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Fenugreek
  • Salt to taste
For the Rice:
  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Tumeric
  • 1 stick Cinnamon

Steps:

  1. If using whole spices, lightly toast the fenugreek and mustard seeds until fragrant.
  2. If using whole spices, grind the toasted fenugreek and mustard seeds using a mortar and pestle.
  3. Add the oil to a large pan. When the oil is heated, add the onions, garlic, chilli, and ginger. Sautee the onions for 10 minutes, stirring often. (Try our )
  4. Add the spices. Stir them in and cook for another 3-4 minutes, stirring often.
  5. Add the tomato paste and cook for another minute or two, stirring often.
  6. Add the chickpeas, stirring them in.
  7. Add the coconut milk. Stir and heat until simmering. Simmer for 12 minutes.
  8. Add all of the ingredients for the rice to a saucepan. Cover and put on low heat to cook until the liquid is all absorbed. About 12 minutes.
  9. (Optional) Before serving the curry, add the coconut flour.
